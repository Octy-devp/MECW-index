ENGELS TO HERMANN SCHLÜTER

IN LONDON

[London], 31 January 1889

Dear Schlüter,

I had hoped to see you and your wife[1] here yesterday. Did Mrs Bernstein come with you? If so, I hope to see her and you both at my house one evening—Sunday at the latest. Kindest regards.

Your

F. Engels
