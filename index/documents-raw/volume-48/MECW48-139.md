ENGELS TO THE EDITOR OF THE *CHICAGOER ARBEITER-ZEITUNG*

[Draft]

Hoboken, 18 September 1888 For your private information

*Chicagoer Arbeiter-Zeitung*

In the course of a short visit to America it has not, unfortunately, proved possible for me to come to Chicago and call on you in person at your office. I should like to say how much I regret this, and remain

Yours very faithfully
