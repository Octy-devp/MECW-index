*To Kautsky in London*

**London, 15 June 1887**

Dear Kautsky,

On what day did you write to Liebknecht about *la* Schack? I've just had a letter from Mrs Liebknecht from which it appears that she, at any rate, knows nothing at all about the matter. Would like to clear up the mystery.

Your

F. E.
