ENGELS TO NIKOLAI DANIELSON

IN ST PETERSBURG

London, 15 July 1889

My dear Sir,

Excuse my ridiculous oversight in not giving you the address of Mr Lafargue.

It is

P. Lafargue

60 Avenue des Champs Elysées

*Le Perreux*

Seine—France.

The book—and the other important one on the same question by Tooke,[1] of which too I happen to have a doublette, will be sent to you tomorrow.

Yours very truly

P. W. Rosher[2]
