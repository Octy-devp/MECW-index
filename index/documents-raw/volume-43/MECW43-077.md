*To Engels in Manchester*

London, 25 September 1868

Dear FRED,

Can you send me £5 by Saturday[1] morning? One of the TRADESMEN just called and told me he was on the verge of bankruptcy and would have great expenses if he had not raised A CERTAIN SUM from HIS CREDITORS by Saturday.

Your

K. M.
