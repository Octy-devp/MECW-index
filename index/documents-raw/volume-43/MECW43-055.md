MARX TO FRIEDRICH LESSNER

IN LONDON

London, 11 August 1868

Dear Lessner,

Because of the orthographic mistakes, I have re-written the whole appeal, enclosed herewith. Now you should copy it in *your own handwriting. Salut.*

Your

K. M.
