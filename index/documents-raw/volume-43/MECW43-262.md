MARX TO SAMUEL DAVENPORT[1]

London, 12 November 1869

1 Modena Villas, Maitland Park,

Haverstock Hill, N. W.

Sir,

I have the honour to enclose a post office order for 2 guineas as my yearly subscription for the Society of Arts.[2]

Yours obediently

Karl Marx
