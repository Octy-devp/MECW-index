MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 7 April 1869

DEAR FRED,

Enclosed letter from Wilhelmchen.?[1] Return after reading. What shall I write him about the *Peasant War[2]* Strohn is at the door, so *addio.*

Your

K. M.
