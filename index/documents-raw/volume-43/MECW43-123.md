MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 6 December 1868

DEAR FRED,

Please send me *£3* by this Wednesday[1] (the day after tomorrow), since the gas bill, etc., are due, and I have promised to pay by then.

*Salut.*

Your

K. M.

So it is Lowe and Bright Co.![2]
