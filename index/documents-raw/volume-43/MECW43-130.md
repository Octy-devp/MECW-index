MARX TO ASHER & CO.[1]

IN LONDON

*[Draft]*

[London, not before 12 December 1868] The only place where a few copies of *Herr Vogt* are still obtainable, is Berlin. At least, there were some to be had a few weeks ago. I shall write there and if a copy can be got, it will be forwarded to 11, Unter den Linden.

Yours truly

K. M.
