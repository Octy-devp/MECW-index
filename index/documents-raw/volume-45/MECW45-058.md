MARX TO MAX OPPENHEIM

IN PRAGUE

Karlsbad, 9 September 1875
Germania

Dear Friend,

I shall be leaving on Saturday (11th) at 3.57 and am due to arrive at the State Railway Station in Prague at 8.50.[1]

I shall be on my own, without any female companion;[2] hence any old bedroom will do.

Many thanks for the *Prager Führer.* With most cordial regards,

Your

K. M.
