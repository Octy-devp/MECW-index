MARX TO ACHILLE LORIA

IN MANTUA

London, 3 December 1879
41 Maitland Park Road, N. W.

Dear Sir,

For reasons of health I was compelled to absent myself from London for a time. On my return today I found awaiting me your letter of 23 November and your work.[1] I hasten to acknowledge receipt and at the same time express my thanks to you.

Yours very truly,

Karl Marx
