MARX TO PYOTR LAVROV[1]

IN LONDON

[London, 21 April 1877]

My dear Friend,

We shall expect you tomorrow (Sunday) for dinner (2 o'clock). In great haste, and with regards from Mrs Marx,

Yours,

K. M.
