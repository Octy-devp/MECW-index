ENGELS TO HERMANN RAMM[1]

IN LEIPZIG

[London, 18 March 1875]

Answered 18/3. *Possibly* 1,000 talers from me, but dependent on circumstances over which I have no control. Shall revert to this as soon as possible.[2]
