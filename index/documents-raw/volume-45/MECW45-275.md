MARX TO ENGELS

IN LONDON

[Ramsgate,] 14 September 1879^52S

Dear FRED,

Tell Tussy that she is invited to 2 o'clock dinner *at Jenny's* on Sunday and to a display of Edgar 'Marcel' Longuet who was today registered a British citizen at Ramsgate.

For Jennychen and suite leave for London tomorrow; ditto Helene[1] to give her SUPPORT.

CHEQUE encashable Monday. I have discovered another letter from Hirsch to myself which belongs in the dossier[2] of the case; I enclose it.

*Salut.*

Your

Moor
