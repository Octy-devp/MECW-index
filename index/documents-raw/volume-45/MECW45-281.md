ENGELS TO JOHANN PHILIPP BECKER

IN GENEVA

London, 24 September 1879

Dear Old Man,

Your postcard^a received. I have taken out a money order for £1 12s. which should come to 40.20 francs, so that you won't be in a hole if the cost of the power of attorney b amounts to a bit more. Marx is back,[1] apparently in the very best of health, so no doubt work on the 2nd volume of *Capital*[2] can now go briskly ahead.

As regards the progress of negotiations with our people in the land of the philistines, I shall let you have further news in due course.[3]

In haste — Your old friend,

F. E.
