[London,] December 19, 1877

41 Maitland Park Road, N. W.

Gentlemen,

Herewith a letter sent me from Breslau[1] for forwarding to you. The sender, Horovitz, though not known to me, has written saying he is a member of the Breslau section of the Social-Democratic Party.

With kind regards,

**Karl Marx**
