MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 9 November 1867

DEAR FRED,

Enclosed from Kugelmann. *Salut.*

Your

Moor

[Postscript from Laura Marx in English]

Dear Engels,

As Mohr's Secretary I am really rather ashamed to send off a letter of this kind.

Yours sincerely

Kakadou[1]
