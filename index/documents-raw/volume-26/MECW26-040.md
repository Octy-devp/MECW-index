Engels addressed this message of greetings to the meeting organised by the Federation of the Centre of the French Workers' Party on the occasion of the 16th anniversary of the Paris Commune. It was read out at the meeting.

London, 18 March, 1887

Citizens,

I am with you in my heart to celebrate the 18 March.

***F. Engels***
