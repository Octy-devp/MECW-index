ENGELS TO PASQUALE MARTIGNETTI

IN BENEVENTO

London, 6 March 1891

Many thanks for *Critica Sociale.*[1] Sig. Avv.[2] Turati had already sent it to me direct and promises to send it regularly. Trust that your prospects are brighter. I am anxious to see the translation of the *Communist Manifesto.*

Warm regards from

Your

F.E.
