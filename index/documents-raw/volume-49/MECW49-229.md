ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN STUTTGART

[London,] 27 June 1892

Our good Schorlemmer died peacefully this morning. I'm going down tomorrow.[1]

There will be an obituary in the *Vorwärts.*[2]

Your

F.E.
