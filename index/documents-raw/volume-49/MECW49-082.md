ENGELS TO HENRI RAVE

IN POITIERS

[Notes]

[London,] 6 March [1891]

To send proof-sheet or two of his translation of Bebel.[1] Has he a publisher? No promise. Might indicate him the chapters[2] which will not be changed or not much so that he might begin. *No promise.*
