ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN LONDON

[London, 8 July 1886]

Safely home 5 9 0—today called to see Guillaume-Schack, but she was out — hence, so far have not had the pleasure of making her ac- quaintance. 592 Schorlemmer is with us — shall you be coming over here tomorrow evening?

Your

F.E.
