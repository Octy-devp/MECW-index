ENGELS TO JOHN LINCOLN MAHON

IN LONDON

[London,] 28 November 1884

122 Regent's Park Road, N.W.

Dear Sir,

I received your note only this morning owing to the No. being stated wrong on the address (132 instead of 122).

If you will be good enough to call on me to-morrow Friday night from 7 to 8, I shall be glad to hear what you have to communicate to me.

Yours truly,

F. Engels
