ENGELS TO KARL KAUTSKY

IN LONDON

[Eastbourne,][1] Friday, 2 July 1886

Many thanks for all the trouble you've taken. Please don't send me, apart from *Nature,* any English papers, or the Manchester paper which arrives on Saturdays, or, after Saturday, *any more* papers at all, apart from the *Sozialdemokrat,* or, after Monday, any more letters, for we shall be home again on Wednesday[2] afternoon. The weather continues magnificent — I await with interest the first election results from the larger towns tomorrow morning. Best wishes from my family to yours.

Your

F.E.
