JENNY MARX TO FREDERICK ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London, end of March 1854]

Dear Mr Engels,

Karl asks you to send Lassalle's address by return. You did not return Lassalle's first letter,[1] which gives it.

Warmest regards from the whole family.

Yours

Jenny Marx
