MARX TO GOTTFRIED KINKEL[1]

IN LONDON [Rough copy]

[London,] 22 July 1852 
5 Sutton Street, Soho 
Office of *The People's Paper* 
To Dr J. G. Kinkel

You are alleged, or so I am informed, to have ventured the following statement before Anneke or others at Cincinnati: 'Marx and Engels...'

^a the young - ^b F. Smitt, *Geschichte des polnischen Aufstandes und Krieges in den Jahren 1830 und 1831*. ^c L. Mieroslawski, *Kritische Darstellung des Feldzuges vom Jahre 1831*. ^f Ernst Dronke

I await your answer by return of post. Silence will be regarded as an admission.

Dr K. M.
