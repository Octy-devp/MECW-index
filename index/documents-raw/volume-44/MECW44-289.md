ENGELS TO EUGEN OSWALD

IN LONDON

[London,] 18 March 1873

122 Regent's Park Road

Dear Oswald,

My very best thanks for the concert tickets you so kindly sent—I have managed in the meantime to sell 2 more of the same sort. I enclose 10/- for them and would ask you to give them to Pumps.[1]

My best regards to MRS and Miss Oswald.

Yours cordially,

F. Engels
