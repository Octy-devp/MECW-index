MARX TO JOHANN PHILIPP BECKER

IN GENEVA

[London, 15 September 1870]

Dear Becker,

I sent a translation[1] to the *Volksstaat* a few days ago, because more urgent. However, *this version* is improved in a number of places.

*Salut*

Your

K. M.
