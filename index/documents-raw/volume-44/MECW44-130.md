MARX TO FRIEDRICH ADOLPH SORGE

IN HOBOKEN

[London,] 5 September 1871

Dear Mr Sorge,

Enclosed the appeal for the REFUGEES.[1] Your mail from America, dated 23 August, arrived today.[2]

Yours sincerely,

Karl Marx
