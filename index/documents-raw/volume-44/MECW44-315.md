MARX TO BENJAMIN LE MOUSSU

IN LONDON

[London, after 3 September 1873]

My dear Le Moussu,

I have considered the matter about which you got in touch with me[1] and shall be obliged if you will come and see me before going into town.

Yours ever,

Karl Marx
