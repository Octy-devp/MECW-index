MARX TO ENGELS

IN LONDON

[Brighton,] 21 August 1871

DEAR FRED,

Time until Wednesday.^3

Jung here since Saturday, goes back today. I shall get some money for the REFUGEES through a (French) priest called Pascal.[1]

*Salut,*

The pen too bad to write.

Your

K. M.
