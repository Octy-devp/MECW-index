ENGELS TO RUDOLF SEIFFERT[1]

IN LEIPZIG

[London, between 15 and 20 January 1873]

31 December [1872]—letter to Liebknecht[2] and Hepner.[3]

6 January [1873] (Fink) a few items of printed matter, No. 38 of *International Herald,* etc.

7 " (Fink) registered letter with article on Prussia.[4]

15 " (ditto) again *International Herald,* 2 Nos., and other printed matter.

Hope everything arrived safely.
