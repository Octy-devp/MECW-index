MARX TO JOHN HALES[1]

IN LONDON

[London,] 14 October 1871

Dear Hales,

You must copy the following Resolution^0 and send it to the daily London papers (the *English* ones; Serraillier sends it to the French papers).

Yours fraternally,

Karl Marx
