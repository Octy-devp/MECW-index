*The document is written in an unknown hand, but signed by Marx.*

To Police Superintendent Geiger

Here.

Sir, I inform you that I have immediately appealed to the Ministry of the Interior against the paper drawn up by you and that I continue to regard myself as before as a citizen of the German Empire.

Cologne, August 5, 1848

Editor-in-Chief of the *Neue Rheinische Zeitung* , Dr. Karl Marx
