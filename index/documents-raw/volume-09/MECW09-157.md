This receipt was made at the time of Marx's trip to North-West Germany and Westphalia in mid-April 1849 with the aim of drawing local workers' associations into the preparations for organising a proletarian party, of establishing closer contacts with the members of the Communist League and democrats and of collecting funds to continue the publication of the *Neue Rheinische Zeitung.*

I acknowledge that I have received an advance of 50 talers from Herr von Frisch.

Dr. K. Marx,

Harburg, May 6
