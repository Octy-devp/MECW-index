ENGELS TO ADELHEID DWORAK AND JULIUS POPP

IN VIENNA

[London, February 1894]

Cordial congratulations 341 from me too, dear Adelheid and Popp. Good examples are, as it were, raining round us thick and fast, and were I not so old and lame a horse, who knows—I might still decide to go into harness again.

Your old

F. Engels
