ENGELS TO STANISLAW MENDELSON

IN LONDON

[London,] 23 April 1895

41 Regent's Park Road, N. W.

My dear Mendelson, If it is convenient, I will call to see you tomorrow, on Wednesday, between 3 and 4 in the afternoon—*weather permitting.* My best wishes to Mme M. and yourself,

From

yours

F. Engels
