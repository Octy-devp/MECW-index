ENGELS TO EDUARD BERNSTEIN

IN LONDON

[London,] 14 November 1894

Dear Ede,

Herewith two sheets,[1] nos. 21/22. Meissner wants to know whether there are any more errata. If you have any to hand, please let me have them; otherwise don't bother; for in that case I shall write to Meissner tomorrow, telling him to close the list. Should you be writing to the Baron *today,* please let him know that I am sending him Ferri's *Socialismo e scienza positiva* with Labriola's notes and at Labriola's request. No hurry about this if your letter has already gone off. Many regards; all well here.

Yours

F. E.
