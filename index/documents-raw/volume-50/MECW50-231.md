ENGELS TO WITOLD JODKO-NARKIEWICZ

IN LONDON

[London,] 14 December 1894

41 Regent's Park Road, N. W.

Dear Jodko,

I certainly would not have the least objection to your translating my article on the peasant question and should be glad if it could also prove helpful to your compatriots.[1]

Yours,

F. Engels

As you see, we have moved house.
