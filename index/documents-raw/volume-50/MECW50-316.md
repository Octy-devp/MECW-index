ENGELS TO EDUARD BERNSTEIN

IN LONDON

Eastbourne, 4 July 1895

4 Royal Parade

Dear Ede,

Letter received. Thanks. So far the same as always, i.e. my mood changes constantly, according to my physical condition. No question of being able to work or even deal with the most urgent correspondence. Laura left yesterday and Louise is back. Like me, she sends her love to you, the children and Gine.[1]

Yours,

F.E.
