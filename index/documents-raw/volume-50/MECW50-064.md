ENGELS TO THOMAS COOK AND SON

IN LONDON

[Eastbourne], after 3 March 1893

Dear Sirs,

Your letter of the 3^rd has been forwarded to me here.[1] The gentleman in question[2] is a young physician and member of the University of Vienna, Austria, where he graduated and practised with distinction.

He has been highly recommended to me by a prominent member of the Austrian Parliament[3] and I have no doubt that you will find him a very desirable customer.
