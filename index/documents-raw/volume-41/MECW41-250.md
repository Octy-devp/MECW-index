MARX TO WILHELM WOLFF

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 4 October 1862

Dear Lupus,

You will have received the *Barmer Zeitung,* misprints and all.[1]

The enclosed cutting from the *Elberfelder Zeitung* was sent me by Siebel, with a note to the effect that the article from the *Banner* was in fact reprinted by the *Niederrheinische,*[2] ditto by the *Märkische Volks-Zeitung* (the latter is published in Hagen). Is Engels back? 457

*Salut.*

Your

K. M.
