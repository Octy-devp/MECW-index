ENGELS TO MARX

IN LONDON

[Manchester, before 12 August 1862]

Herewith the bill on Baron Artful.[1] *You must* advise him of it, date, day when due, etc., also that he will receive the money on due date and that there will be an extension till 1 Jan., when I shall remit him the difference of £15.

Your

F. E.[2]
