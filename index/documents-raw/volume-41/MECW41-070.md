MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 28 May 1860

Dear Engels,

The enclosed is from Lassalle.[1] Let me know *by return* what you think I should write and tell him *re* Fischel.

I do not fall in with his suggestion about Berlin. Nothing yet from Lommel.

Your

K. M.
