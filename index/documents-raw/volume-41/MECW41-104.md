MARX TO ENGELS

IN MANCHESTER

[London,] 13 September 1860

Dear Engels,

I hope to get an article from you for Saturday.[1] If at all possible. Herewith Zimmermannes legal opinion on the questions I put to him. *(To be returned* to me next week.)

Also, for your amusement, a copy of a letter given to me by Eccarius.

Your

K. M.
