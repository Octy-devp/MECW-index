MARX TO ENGELS 3 6'

IN MANCHESTER

[London, 26 January 1859]

DEAR Frederick,

T h e *£2* safely received; the manuscript[1] gone off; wrote ECONOMICAL REVIEW for the *Tribune* yesterday. More tomorrow, and SOMETHING VERY AMUSING TOO.

Your

K. M.
