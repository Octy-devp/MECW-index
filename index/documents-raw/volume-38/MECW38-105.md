This is a postscript by Marx to Conrad Schramm's letter to Joseph Weydemeyer of 8 January 1850

Dear Weydemeyer,

I [enclose] the pawn-ticket herewith. Would you be so kind as to renew it and to deduct the cost from the subscriptions.[1] Kindest regards to your wife[2] from mine, likewise to yourself.

K. Marx
