MARX TO CARL PEARSON 88

IN LONDON

[London,] 15 February 1881
41 Maitland Park, N.W.

My dear Sir,

I should have answered before but for great pressure of work dur- ing the last days.

Will you give me the pleasure to call on me on Thursday next,[1] 8 o'clock p. m.?

Yours truly,

Karl Marx
