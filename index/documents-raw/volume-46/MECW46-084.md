MARX TO ENGELS

AT BRIDLINGTON QUAY

[London,] 19 August 1881, 11.30 p.m.

Mama and Helen[1] have JUST arrived via Folkestone, after stopping at Boulogne.

What I didn't write and tell you — Longuet and little Harry are very ill. Nothing but misfortune in the family at the moment.

*Salut.*

K.M.
