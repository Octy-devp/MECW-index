ENGELS TO JOHANN PHILIPP BECKER

IN GENEVA

London, 28 March 1881

122 Regent's Park Road, N. W.

Dear Old Man,

I shall send you some money the instant you put me in a position to do so by letting me *have your new address.* For I have to produce this, and such addresses as I find in the *Précurseur* do not seem to me really fit to be produced if they are not to create difficulties.
