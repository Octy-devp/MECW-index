MARX TO PYOTR LAVROV

IN PARIS

[Argenteuil,] 16 August 1881

My dear LavrofF,

I must leave to-morrow and will therefore have no occasion to see[1] you again for this time. But having once found my way to Paris, I shall put in my appearance from time to time.

*En attendant au revoir Tout à vous*

K. Marx
