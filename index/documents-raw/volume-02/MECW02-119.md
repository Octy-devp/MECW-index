Certificate of Conduct

The bearer of this certificate, one-year Volunteer Bombardier Friedrich Engels of the 12th Foot Company of the Guards Artillery Brigade, born in Barmen, Elberfeld Division, Düsseldorf Government District, aged twenty-one years and ten months, having served one year, has conducted himself very well during his period of service in respect both of morals and service, as is herewith duly certified by

v. Wedell,

Captain and Company Commander
